DAVIDE LOMBARDI

A square in the heart of Pisa
Pisa (IT)

The project for “Pisa Conference Room” presented by the clear desire to create a “silent” architecture respectful of the place where it fits and its main characteristics: a light, balanced, and at the same time lively, space an interesting pole for the city and those who live as tourists or daily.The green roof then becomes a walk usable by visitors, an architectural promenade from which to enjoy new views and new views of the Campo dei Miracoli and its monuments. This choice is also well related to the desire to camouflage the new building in the area: Campo dei Miracoli is characterized by the presence of large green lawns, taken here to define the outdoor area.The particular dynamic of the building itself is flanked by the design of the external space: a slight difference in height creates a downward space, bounded by the main circular hall of the Conference Hall, which leads visitors directly into the entrance reception, located below the line of the campaign plan.
