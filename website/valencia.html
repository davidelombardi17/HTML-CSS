DAVIDE LOMBARDI

College retraining
Valencia (ES)

The main axis of the project consists of two volumes dedicated to classrooms. Freeing the ground floor, leaving one cleaner direction, spaces of higher quality relationship and better lighting. Starting by a climate analysis of the site, our premise for the improvement of energy efficiency is natural light, only this point is a big change with respect to the current state, with which less electrical expenditure. For this we propose the opening of the interior that is illuminated by a greater skylight, in addition the classrooms inside illuminate glazed partitions. At the outside, and due to the great sunlight, we modify the facade gaps making them more slender, so obtain greater illumination, but less radiation, since the facade is composed of two parts, the interior formedby an external SATE system, followed by a hollow brick factory and an interior finish of plasterboard laminate. The second skin, exterior and ventilated, composed of a superposition of matted glass panels and low emissive, helping the ventilation.
