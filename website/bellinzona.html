DAVIDE LOMBARDI

FFS factory regeneration
Bellinzona (CH)

Object of the retraining are Bellinzona’s train factory, characterized by spacius blank spaces, because they were destinated to the train fixing. Aim of the project has been the comunication of this void, without filling the ground floor of the Cathedral, allowing the rails to cross the building. The only volumes protruding to the square are two and they have similar funcion (to give a new view over the Cathedral). Puropose are, by the way, different. The first one works as diaphragm, in this way the visitor doesn’t perceived immediatly the whole space. The second one is a reference: allow the visitors to better understand the space with a volume more similar to those they’re used for, comparing it with the void, wich is not a frame anymore, but protagonist.
